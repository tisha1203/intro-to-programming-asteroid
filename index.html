About

Tisha possesses 35 years of professional experience that incorporates auditing, accounting, event planning, marketing, fund development, human resources, graphic design and entrepreneurial endeavors. Tisha is proficient in Canva, Photoshop, InDesign, Splice video editing, PowerPoint, Word, Excel, Outlook, Dynamics CRM, QuickBooks, Wave, E-Recruit, MailChimp, Constant Contact, Cvent, Hubspot, Basecamp and ConvertKit.
